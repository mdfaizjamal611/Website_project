Supply and Installation

Our integrated supply and installation services offer comprehensive solutions for clients seeking a seamless process. With efficient procurement and deployment, we manage the entire supply chain to ensure timely delivery and effective project execution. Our turnkey approach provides end-to-end solutions, custom-tailored to meet specific client requirements. Some areas where we offer supply and installation are –
➢ Installation, Commissioning of Field Instrumentation, Electrical/LV/MV Panel, Metering Skids.
➢ Supply of Painting/Blasting/NDE/Welding Consumables, Hand tools, Torque Wrenches, Field instrumentation
➢ Pressure Gauges, Control System, Analyzers, Cables, VFD. Vibration & Condition Monitoring Devices, Power Management & control system & Other Electrical
➢ Installation & Fabrication of Tanks, Piping & Structures

Installation, Commissioning of Field Instrumentation, Electrical/LV/MV Panel, Metering Skids.

1. Field Instrumentation Installation: Planning and execution of the installation process for field instrumentation, including sensors, transmitters, and control devices.
2. Electrical Panel Installation (LV/MV): Implementation of electrical panels for both low voltage (LV) and medium voltage (MV) systems, ensuring proper wiring, connections, and safety measures.
3. Commissioning Procedures: Development and execution of commissioning procedures to verify the functionality and performance of installed instrumentation, panels, and metering skids.
4. Metering Skids Integration: Integration of metering skids into the overall system, involving the installation and calibration of flow meters, pressure gauges, and other measurement devices.
5. Quality Control and Documentation: Implementation of rigorous quality control measures during installation and commissioning, accompanied by comprehensive documentation to ensure compliance with standards and specifications.

Supply of Painting/Blasting/NDE/Welding Consumables, Hand tools, Torque Wrenches, Field instrumentation

1. Comprehensive Supply Services: Provision of a wide range of painting, blasting, NDE (Non-Destructive Evaluation), welding consumables, hand tools, torque wrenches, and field instrumentation for diverse industrial needs.
2. Quality Assurance: Ensuring the supply of high-quality consumables and tools that meet industry standards, promoting reliability and performance in various applications.
3. Product Diversity: Offering a diverse inventory of products, including paints, abrasives, NDE equipment, welding consumables, and precision instruments, to cater to different aspects of industrial operations.
4. Timely Delivery: Commitment to timely and efficient delivery services, minimizing downtime and ensuring that clients have the necessary supplies when needed.
5. Customized Solutions: Providing tailored solutions to meet specific project requirements, offering a range of products suitable for different surface treatments, welding applications, and instrumentation needs.

Pressure Gauges, Control System, Analyzers, Cables, VFD. Vibration & Condition Monitoring Devices, Power Management & control system & Other Electrical

1. Pressure Gauges and Analyzers: Supply of accurate pressure gauges and analyzers for precise monitoring and control, ensuring reliable measurement of fluid and gas pressures.
2. Control Systems and VFDs: Provision of advanced control systems and Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs) for efficient and adaptable management of motors and industrial processes.
3. Vibration and Condition Monitoring Devices: Supplying devices for vibration analysis and condition monitoring to assess the health of machinery and prevent potential failures.
4. Cables and Electrical Components: Delivery of high-quality cables and various electrical components essential for powering and connecting equipment within industrial systems.
5. Power Management and Control Systems: Providing comprehensive solutions for power management and control, optimizing energy efficiency and ensuring smooth operation of electrical systems.

Installation & Fabrication of Tanks, Piping & Structures

1. Tank Installation and Fabrication: Expertise in the installation and fabrication of storage tanks, encompassing precise engineering, welding, and quality control processes.
2. Piping System Fabrication: Proficient fabrication of piping systems, involving precise cutting, welding, and assembly to meet design specifications and industry standards.
3. Structural Fabrication: Skilled fabrication of industrial structures, ensuring the construction of robust and compliant frameworks for various applications.
4. Comprehensive Installation Services: End-to-end installation services for tanks, piping, and structures, including site preparation, assembly, and integration into existing systems.
5. Quality Assurance and Compliance: Adherence to stringent quality assurance measures during fabrication and installation, ensuring that tanks, piping, and structures meet safety, durability, and regulatory standards.

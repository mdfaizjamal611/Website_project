Design and detailing

Our design and detailing services encompass a meticulous approach to engineering, ensuring precision in the creation of structures, piping systems, and pressure vessels. Leveraging advanced tools such as CAD and 3D modeling, we focus on accurate representations and comprehensive detailing. Our commitment extends to providing robust and compliant designs, adhering to industry standards and specifications. Some areas where we offer Design and Details are –
➢ Skid based EPC projects
➢ Design & Detailing of Pressure Vessel, Boiler, Piping & Structure
➢ Piping Prefabrication Spooling Activities
➢ Piping Construction Management Services Data Base
➢ Piping Test Pack Preparation & Handling

Skid based EPC projects

1. Turnkey EPC Solutions: Provision of skid-based Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) projects, offering end-to-end solutions from design to installation.
2. Modular Skid Systems: Design and fabrication of modular skid systems for efficient and compact integration of various processes, equipment, or components.
3. Comprehensive Project Management: Holistic project management covering engineering design, procurement of materials, construction, and commissioning of skid-based systems.
4. Customized Solutions: Tailoring skid-based EPC projects to meet specific client requirements, ensuring flexibility and adaptability to diverse industrial applications.
5. Quality Control and Compliance: Rigorous quality control measures and adherence to industry standards, ensuring the reliability and safety of skid-based EPC projects throughout their lifecycle.

Design & Detailing of Pressure Vessel, Boiler, Piping & Structure

1. Pressure Vessel Design: Expertise in designing pressure vessels, ensuring compliance with industry codes and standards such as ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code.
2. Boiler Design and Detailing: Comprehensive design and detailing services for boilers, covering structural integrity, thermal efficiency, and compliance with safety regulations.
3. Piping System Engineering: Design and detailing of efficient and safe piping systems, considering fluid dynamics, stress analysis, and compatibility with various materials.
4. Structural Design and Detailing: Designing robust and compliant industrial structures, with a focus on structural integrity, load-bearing capacity, and safety.
5. CAD and 3D Modeling: Utilization of Computer-Aided Design (CAD) and 3D modeling tools for accurate and detailed representations of pressure vessels, boilers, piping, and structures.

Piping Prefabrication Spooling Activities

1. Detailed Spooling Plans: Development of detailed spooling plans for piping prefabrication, outlining the arrangement and assembly of pipe spools to ensure efficient installation.
2. Material Selection and Cutting: Expertise in selecting suitable materials and precision cutting for fabrication of piping spools, optimizing material usage and minimizing waste.
3. Welding and Assembly: Skilled welding and assembly of prefabricated piping spools, ensuring high-quality joints and conformity with design specifications.
4. Quality Inspection: Implementation of rigorous quality inspection during and after prefabrication, including visual checks and non-destructive testing to detect and rectify defects.
5. On-Time Delivery: Commitment to on-time delivery of prefabricated spools, facilitating efficient installation and minimizing construction timelines.

Piping Construction Management Services Data Base

1. Comprehensive Data Management: Development and management of a comprehensive database for piping construction projects, containing vital information on materials, schedules, and progress.
2. Project Planning and Tracking: Utilization of the database for project planning, tracking milestones, and ensuring that construction activities align with timelines and budgetary constraints.
3. Resource Allocation: Efficient allocation of resources, including manpower, equipment, and materials, through the database to optimize construction workflows.
4. Quality Control and Compliance: Integration of quality control measures into the database, ensuring adherence to industry standards, codes, and regulatory requirements.
5. Reporting and Analysis: Generation of detailed reports and analysis using the database, providing insights into project performance, identifying areas for improvement, and facilitating informed decision-making.

Piping Test Pack Preparation & Handling

1. Test Pack Compilation: Preparation of comprehensive test packs containing all relevant documentation, inspection records, and testing procedures for piping systems.
2. Quality Assurance Documentation: Inclusion of quality assurance documents, such as material certificates, welding procedure specifications, and inspection reports, within the test pack.
3. Testing Procedures and Protocols: Detailed documentation of testing procedures, including hydrostatic testing, non-destructive testing (NDT), and pressure testing, ensuring thorough quality assessments.
4. Regulatory Compliance: Ensuring that the test pack aligns with industry standards, client specifications, and regulatory requirements for piping systems.
5. Efficient Handling and Distribution: Streamlining the handling and distribution of test packs to relevant stakeholders, facilitating organized and transparent documentation throughout the project lifecycle.
